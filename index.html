Local SEO Services FAQs

SEO, in general, is the process of getting your website to rank in Google search results for relevant search queries. Local SEO does that, too; but with a more localized focus - as its name implies.

Local SEO doesn’t just focus exclusively on your business website. For this strategy, you also need to focus on your Google My Business profile page. Why is that? Because that’s what Google shows for the majority of locally-focused search queries. This is doubly important because it helps improve ranking, but it also gives you additional visibility with a map that shows where businesses are located.

Let’s go back to our restaurant example. When you search for a local business like Thai restaurants on Google, there will be a map in the listings that includes the restaurant names, addresses, phone numbers, and reviews. This will be right at the top of the first page of the search results. And that’s right where you want to be!

All of this information is coming from each business’s Google My Business page. The main takeaway here is that in order to be competitive in local SEO, you need to optimize both your website and your Google My Business profile page.
